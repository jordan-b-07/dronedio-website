About Us

Drone Dio is the University of Wollongong's official student drone club, founded with a vision to advance drone technology through hands-on projects, research, and international competition.

As the first team from Australia to represent in the SUAS (Student Unmanned Aerial Systems) competition in the USA, we aim to foster talent, encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, and push boundaries in aerial innovation. Our mission is to empower students with real-world experience in drone development — from hardware and software integration to AI, computer vision, and autonomous navigation — while creating impactful solutions for civil, environmental, and industrial challenges.

Our Vision

At Drone Dio, our vision is to make the University of Wollongong a hub of drone innovation that's recognized across Australia and beyond. We aim to lead the future of aerial systems through groundbreaking research, student-led projects, and industry partnerships.

By building a bridge between the classroom and real-world application, we empower students to create technologies that solve modern-day challenges — from disaster response and agriculture to environmental monitoring and infrastructure inspection.
